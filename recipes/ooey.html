Ooey Gooey Cake

Description

A little difficult to make but well worth the effort. Ooey gooey cake is a delicious treat.

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Mix cake mix and 2 eggs together in a bowl.
  4. Add butter and vanilla extract; mix until batter is smooth and thick.
  5. Spread batter into the prepared baking dish.
  6. Beat confectioners' sugar, cream cheese, and 2 eggs together in a bowl until smooth; spread over batter.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until sides of cake are pulled away from dish and browned, 25 to 35 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle more confectioners' sugar over cooled cake.
